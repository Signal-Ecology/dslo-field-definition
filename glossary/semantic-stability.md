# Semantic Stability (DSLO Glossary)

## Definition
Semantic stability is the property of a system whose meaning remains constant across time, iteration, refactoring, and agent‑mediated change.

It is the central objective of DSLO.

## Purpose
- Ensure long‑term coherence of domain meaning.
- Prevent degradation of business intent.
- Maintain structural and semantic integrity.
- Enable safe autonomous evolution.

## Properties
- Time‑stable  
- Iteration‑stable  
- Agent‑stable  
- Context‑stable  
- Invariant‑anchored  

## Stability Mechanisms
- Semantic Stability Protocols  
- Invariant Gates  
- Deterministic Substrate Execution  
- Drift Detection and Prevention
